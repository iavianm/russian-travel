What haven't we seen there?

According to VTsIOM polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% plan to spend their vacation in their native country. Like what haven't we seen here at home? In fact, Russia — is the whole universe with the tender sea of the south, dense forests of the Sayan Mountains, and harsh ice of the Putorana plateau. And still it is possible to see all these beauties without millions on the account, the passport and many hours of flights. Like Vera Bashmakova — a brave young mother who took her three children in an  armful, put them into her «Lada» and drove 20 thousand kilometers across her native country. We chose and described some interesting places worthy of your vacation.

Kolsky

Almost the whole peninsula is beyond the Polar Circle. Saami tundra, from which to the south — taiga, and north — the Arctic Ocean pretending to be the Barents Sea.

Maybe you ve watched Zvyagintsev and even heard the story of the Arctic Festival in Teriberka. Maybe the word «Khibiny»didn't stay under the snow of your school memories of geography lessons. Perhaps, you have not wondered about the ultra-deep well penetrating the Earth's crust, and apatites made you feel very apathetic. But your dream of seeing the northern lights is starting to come true with a ticket to Murmansk.

Altai —one of the most& beautiful places in Russia. First of all because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge you'll see pine-clad slopes, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows&of&the&car,you&can&see&the&invisible&wonderful&of&the&country —mountain&air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you can see the variety of local flora and fauna. Moose roam in the Altai forests, eagles fly over the ridges, and roe deer graze on the plains. And famous manul — also inhabitants of the Altai region.

Winter Baikal

URL

https://vk.com/baikalmile

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many people also know that it is the largest source of fresh water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, that's all true. But Baikal is also an ideal place for skiing competitions. It's a sport where the skier straps himself to a motorcycle, and the tandem tries to get as much speed as possible on the ice. In March 2019 at the Festival «Baikal Mile» the world record — 197.011 km/h was set.

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru/

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch that forms the taiga does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here, it reaches up to 30 meters  — the forests of Karelian national parks have never known an axe because of impassable bogs. Some pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch a living creature that saw the sun before Ivan the Terrible saw it. You won't find a trail in a virgin forest for a hundred kilometers. And on the sparse paths, the trees a few feet off the ground are marked with bear claws. So everyone knows who's boss.
